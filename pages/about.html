About ATEC

Indigenous Skills & Employment Training across the Secwepemc and Nlaka'pamux Nations.

Leading the way towards a better you!

SNTC ISET is the Service Canada Indigenous Skills & Employment Training Program (ISETP) agreement holder contracted to implement the ISETP Agreement for Indigenous people.


About Stories Matter: A Qualitative Study Reaffirming the Relevance of Case Studies and Stories on Indigenous Skills Employment and Training The Shuswap Nation Tribal Council Indigenous Skills Employment and Training Program (SNTC ISET) was signed in 2019. Its predecessor was the Aboriginal Skills Employment and Training Strategy I and II, the Aboriginal Human Resources Development Agreement, and the Regional Bilateral Agreement. The program has been in place since April 1, 1996.


Located in the Secwepemc and Nlaka'pamux Nations, including the following First Nations communities:
