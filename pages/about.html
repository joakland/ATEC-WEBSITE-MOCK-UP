About ATEC

Indigenous Skills & Employment Training across the Secwepemc and Nlaka'pamux Nations.

Leading the way towards a better you!

Shuswap Nation Tribal Council (SNTC) manages the Indigenous Skills & Employment Training (ISET) program by providing employment services through our local Aboriginal Training and Employment Centers (ATEC).

We aim to support skill enhancement and assist in securing meaningful employment.

This year, ISET supported:

  • 452 Indigenous clients through ISET-funded interventions
  • 58 Daycare Seats in On-Reserve Daycare Centers
  • 3 On-Reserve Daycare Centers with Repairs & Renovations

Located in the Secwepemc and Nlaka'pamux Nations, including the following First Nations communities:

  • Adams Lake
  • Bonaparte
  • High Bar
  • Little Shuswap
  • Neskonlith
