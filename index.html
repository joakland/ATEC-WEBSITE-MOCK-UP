Aboriginal Training & Employment Centre

Supporting Indigenous individuals across the
Secwepemc and Nlaka’pamux nations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and support Indigenous client success in training and employment.



Our Vision

We envision resilent and empowered Indigenous people thriving in fulfilling careers.

Our Values

~ Welcoming ~ Respectful ~ Culturally Safe ~ Flexible ~ Integrity ~ Collaborative ~

Nations Represented

We are dedicated to empowering all First Nations individuals both on reserves and in urban areas of the Secwepemc and Nlaka’pamux Nations.

Community Partnerships

Through our partnerships, we continue to celebrate achievements, promote inclusive hiring, and support community events that highlight Indigenous leadership and talent. Our shared goals have enabled us to create meaningful pathways for education, employment, and community development.
